Heart Disease Prediction Model

Heart disease is one of the biggest causes of mobidity and mortality among the population of the world. Prediction of cardiovascular disease is regarded as one of the most important subjects in the section of clinical data analysis.

This Web Based Application uses Machine Learning Algorithm to predict whether a person is suffering from heart disease or not using one of the most used dataset - Cleveland Heart Disease Dataset from UCI Repository. [Note that this model is 85% accurate]
